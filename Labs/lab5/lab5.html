  1. In Person learning is better than remote learning because:
    • You don't have to wait for an answer when you ask your teacher something. In online learning we would have to wait one day or more for a reply, but with in person learning you can get an instant reply.
    • You are more likely to pay attention. With online learning, it is easy to get distracted since there is no one there to tell you what to do.
    • It is easier to understand a lesson since the teacher is there to explain to you anything you did not understand.
  1. Remote learning is better than in person learning because:
    • Students can go at their own pace. Since most of the lectures are recorded, students are able to go back and listen to anyparts they missed.
    • More environmentally healthy. Since we turn in assignments digitally, we don't need to use papers as much, therefore less trees are cut down.
    • The schedule is more flexible. With remote learning you can wake up later and turn in assignments after school hours.
